Welcome to DJS ISACA

DJS ISACA is a student-driven chapter focused on raising awareness about the importance of cybersecurity in both personal and professional spaces.
We aim to empower students with practical skills through workshops, competitions, and collaborations, bridging the gap between learning and real-world challenges.
Our goal is to foster a safe digital community, promote ethical practices, and inspire innovation on campus.
Join us in building a more secure and resilient future!

DJS BUILDSPACE

DJS Buildspace is a student-led tech club at D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering (DJSCE) that brings together students passionate about building and experimenting with the latest technologies.
The club is especially known for its focus on Web3, but it also actively explores areas like machine learning, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, backend and frontend.
What sets DJS Buildspace apart is its hands-on approach-members don't just learn theory, they actually get to work on real projects, participate in regular mentoring sessions, and participate in hackathons
with their mentors that challenge and inspire them to apply their skills.The community is supportive and energetic, with experienced members always ready to guide newcomers,
making it easy for anyone to jump in regardless of their background.Through regular events, mentorship, and a strong sense of camaraderie, DJS Buildspace has become
a trusted community for anyone at DJSCE who wants to stay ahead in tech, collaborate on exciting ideas, and be part of a forward-thinking group of innovators.
